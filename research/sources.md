# Newsletter & Email Marketing Sources for B2B SaaS

A curated list of 10 experts and YouTube channels focused on newsletter strategy, email marketing, and B2B SaaS growth.

---

## 1. Russell Brunson

- **YouTube Channel:** [https://www.youtube.com/@RussellBrunson](https://www.youtube.com/@RussellBrunson)
- **Why Selected:** Founder of ClickFunnels and one of the most influential voices in direct-response email marketing. His frameworks around email sequences and list monetization are widely adopted in SaaS funnels.
- **Content Type:** Funnel strategy, email copywriting, list building, sales automation, and direct-response marketing tutorials.

- **YouTube Channel:** [https://www.youtube.com/@HubSpot](https://www.youtube.com/@HubSpot)
- **Why Selected:** HubSpot is the de facto standard for B2B inbound marketing and CRM-integrated email campaigns. Their channel covers email marketing best practices grounded in data from thousands of B2B companies.
- **Content Type:** Email marketing tutorials, campaign strategy, B2B lead nurturing, marketing automation, and platform walkthroughs.
